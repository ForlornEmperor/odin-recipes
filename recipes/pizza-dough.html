Pizza Dough

A recipe for pizza dough that i have peen perfecting for several years.

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 cups 00 flour "any all purpose flour will work"
  • 1-1/2 cups warm water around 104 degrees farenheit
  • 1/3 cup melted vegetable shortening "crisco"
  • 1 tbs salt
  • 2 tbs powdered sugar
  • 2 tbs bread yeast "different types of yeast have different flavor profiles, so choose one that suits your personal preferences."

Cooking instructions

Preparation

  1. Mix warm water and powdered sugar together, then add yeast. Allow yeast time to activate.
  2. Once yeast has activated, add all ingredients to stand mixer and mix for 10 minutes reserve 1 cup flour to add in incramentally later
  3. Add in flour in small amounts untill dough ceases to cling to sides of mixing bowl, but still is tacky.
  4. Coat new stailess steel bowl with olive oil and place dough inside the bowl and cover with aluminum foil.
  5. Float bowl in warm water and allow dough to rise
  6. Once dough has doubled in size fold dough, re cover bowl and allow to rise a second time.
  7. Once dough has doubled in size a second time remove it from the bowl devide, and roll it into two separate balls.
  8. Allow dough to rise in fridge for at least 12 hours prior to cooking."its ok to cook the dough immediatly, but it does rise and taste a lot better if you allow it to proof in the fridge before cooking"

Cooking

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees farenheit.
  2. remove dough from fridge and allow it to reach 50ish degrees before flatening.
  3. roll out dough, dock, then toss dough to large crust size.
  4. add desired toppings and cook 6 minutes at 500 degrees farenheit.
  5. Once the six minutes has elapsed set oven to broil, and pull pizza out when desired level of browning has been achieved.
