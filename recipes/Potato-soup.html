Potato Soup

A recipe that i have been perfecting for several years

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs yukon gold potatos
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup water "to be used later to thin soup to desired consistency"
  • 4 serrano peppers
  • 2 shallots
  • 1/2 stick salted butter
  • 6 strips bacon "for topping"
  • 1 cup shredded cipolte gouda
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1.5 tbs salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/8 tsp ancho chile powder

Cooking Instructions

Prep Ingredients

  • Dice potatos into small pieces, and boil in salted water untill soft then set aside.
  • Thinly dice serrano peppers and shallots, set aside in a small bowl.
  • Crush garlic cloves with garlic press into small bowl.
  • slice bacon into 1 inch wide pieces

Cooking

  1. cook bacon untill crispy in a large pan.
  2. remove bacon and leave behind 1 tbs grease and add butter,shallots,serrano peppers and garic.
  3. Once shalots are soft add flour to make roux.
  4. In large pot, add roux, heavy whipping cream, chicken broth, seasonings,and potatos and heat untill boiling.
  5. Remove 6 cups of soup to blender and puree mixture, then add back to rest of soup.
  6. Add gouda, reduce heat to simmer and cook 15 minutes.
  7. If soup is too thick add water untill it is at the desired consistency.
  8. Remove from heat and allow to cool before serving.
